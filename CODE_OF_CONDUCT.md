# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

In the interest of fostering an open, inclusive, and trustworthy community, we pledge to make participation in Veriglob a harassment-free experience for everyone.

We are committed to creating a welcoming environment regardless of:

- Experience level
- Gender identity and expression
- Sexual orientation
- Disability
- Personal appearance
- Race, ethnicity, nationality
- Religion or lack thereof

---

## Our Standards

Examples of behavior that contributes to a positive environment include:

- Using welcoming and inclusive language
- Being respectful of differing viewpoints
- Gracefully accepting constructive criticism
- Focusing on what is best for the community

Examples of unacceptable behavior include:

- Harassment, insults, or derogatory comments

## Our Responsibilities

Project maintainers are responsible for:

- Clarifying standards of acceptable behavior
- Taking appropriate corrective action when necessary
- Protecting the community from harmful conduct
